How can we help you?

Electrical Installations

We develop assemblies and wiring for all types of electrical infrastructure projects, ensuring energy efficiency and full compliance with NEC 2014.

Technical Scope of Service:

  • Support and Conduit: Installation of cable trays, EMT/IMC/RMC piping, and metal duct systems.
  • Power Wiring: Laying and interconnection of large gauge electrical conductors for medium and low voltage.
  • Service Drops: Installation and configuration of service drops in 480 VAC, 208 VAC, 120 VAC, and other specific voltage levels.
  • Panels and Transformers: Installation, anchoring, and wiring of general distribution panels (sub-panels) and dry or oil-filled transformers.
  • Lighting Circuits: Installation of commercial and industrial lighting systems with high-efficiency LED technology.
  • Motors and Water Pumps: Connection, parameterization, and maintenance of industrial electric motors and water pumping systems.
  • Carbon Monoxide (CO) Detection: Design and installation of safety systems for CO detection in parking lots and enclosed spaces.
Request this Service

Design and Assembly of Control Panels

We automate and control your industrial processes through the design and assembly of high-precision electrical panels tailored to your needs.

Technical Scope of Service:

  • Electrical Plan Design: Preparation of single-line and multi-line diagrams, as well as command and control schemes using engineering CAD software.
  • Panel Assembly: Physical distribution, mounting, and wiring of components in the cabinet (contactors, thermal relays, PLCs, frequency inverters, fuses, and protections).
  • Standardized Marking and Labeling: Clear identification of cables and components using durable industrial labels, facilitating future maintenance.
  • Workshop Testing (FAT): Exhaustive operational tests prior to dispatch to ensure correct logical and electrical functioning.
  • Installation and Commissioning: Physical anchoring in the plant, field connection, on-site operational tests (SAT), and technical handover.
Request this Service

Cleanroom Facilities

We adapt critical manufacturing spaces in the medical and electronic industries, complying with the sterility and order standards your plant requires.

Technical Scope of Service:

  • Structured Cabling: Data networks (Cat6/Cat6A), clean telecommunications conduits, and professional rack dressing.
  • Work Bubbles: Structural assembly and electrical installation for isolation bubbles or specific device assembly areas.
  • Power Drops: Drop connections from the ceiling to workbenches, integrating regulated electrical outlets, data, and mechanical services.
  • Compressed Air: Specialized pneumatic piping of stainless steel or aluminum with quick couplings for pneumatic tools at workstations.
  • Workbench Conditioning: Installation of integrated power strips, localized lighting, and ergonomic support for medical stations.
Request this Service

Electrical Maintenance

We prevent unexpected stoppages in your production through diagnostic routines and high-engineering predictive and preventive maintenance.

Technical Scope of Service:

  • Thermographic Inspection: Identification of hot spots and false contacts in distribution panels using thermal cameras, issuing a detailed report.
  • Preventive Maintenance: Tightening and torque of terminals with torque wrenches, internal cleaning of cabinets by vacuuming/dielectric solvents.
  • 24/7 Corrective Service: Diagnosis and repair of emergency electrical faults, replacement of defective contactors, switches, and burnt wiring.
  • Electrical Measurements: Recording of load parameters (current, voltage, phase unbalance) to verify the health status of the installation.
Request this Service

Building Maintenance (Facilities)

We provide general support to the civil installations, plumbing, and technical supply logistics of your plant or corporate building.

Technical Scope of Service:

  • Minor Civil Works: Wall repairs, industrial floor painting or safety demarcations, and installation of light metal structures.
  • Plumbing and Hydraulics: Preventive maintenance of drinking water networks, drains, leak repair, maintenance of flushometers and commercial faucets.
  • Spare Parts and Materials Management: Quoting service, quick purchase, and direct delivery to the plant of critical electromechanical spare parts and technical supplies.

Personnel Subcontracting (Staffing & Handyman)

Make your operating costs flexible by relying on specialized, trained technical personnel ready to join your maintenance team.

Technical Scope of Service:

  • Technicians through Professional Services: Qualified personnel (electricians, electromechanics) by the hour, day, or week for expansion projects or plant shutdowns.
  • On-Site Handyman Service: Fixed or periodic assignment of a general maintenance technician to perform routine preventive maintenance of building infrastructure.
  • Compliance with Labor Obligations: FHS handles the total employer-employee management, insurance, and occupational safety induction (HSE), mitigating legal risk for your company.
